Day 3

  • LRT 2
    Adrian Francisco A. Salda: Sir Adrian taught us about train operations and emphasized safety precautions for passengers. He highlighted the do's and don'ts for a safe journey. He also demonstrated their software, showing how they track trains and monitor their status. Additionally, he explained the technical details of how trains run and the role of the rails.
  • National Museum of the Philippines
    Berna Romulo Puyat (Tour Guide): She guided us through the National Museum of the Philippines, sharing captivating insights about the exhibits and bringing the country's rich history and culture to life. Her enthusiasm and knowledge made the tour both educational and engaging.
